About Us

CineHub is a Canadian entertainment company headquartered in Toronto, Ontario.

Through its operating subsidiary CinHub Entertainment LP, CinHub operates 165 theatres making it the largest movie theatre chain across Canada. The company operates theatres under numerous brands, including CineHub Cinemas, CineHub Odeon, SilverCity, Galaxy Cinemas, Cinema City, Famous Players, Scotiabank Theatres and CineHub VIP Cinemas.
