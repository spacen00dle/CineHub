About HubPlex

is a Canadian entertainment company headquartered in Toronto, Ontario.

Through its operating subsidiary HubPlex Entertainment LP, HubPlex operates 165 theatres making it the largest movie theatre chain across Canada. The company operates theatres under numerous brands, including HubPlex Cinemas, HubPlex Odeon, SilverCity, Galaxy Cinemas, Cinema City, Famous Players, Scotiabank Theatres and HubPlex VIP Cinemas.

HubPlex also owns and operates multiple brands for entertainment (such as UltraAVX, Xscape Entertainment Centre, Player One Amusement Group) and restaurants (such as OutTakes and Poptopia). It is also a joint partner in the Scene loyalty program with Scotiabank.
